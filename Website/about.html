Why you should choose us?

Niramay is an application that will help you stay healthy. In this application user can store his information and by supervising the information application will give tips and suggestions for better lives. Also user can search for a disease and can find the detail information regarding it.It will also provides the facility to check covid vaccine availability and step tracker system too.It will also provide medication tips for the respective disease along with contact details of hospitals which provide facilities to cure it.It will also give an alert to user regarding to health.
